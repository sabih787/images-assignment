The Koenigsegg Jesko is a high-performance sports car introduced at the 2019 Geneva Motor Show. It is named as a tribute to the company founder's father, Jesko von Koenigsegg. There are two variations of the car: the "Absolut" and the "Attack". The Jesko features a turbocharged 5.0-liter V8 engine that can produce close to 1,603 horsepower on E85 and is capable of top speeds of 278 mph

Ferrari is a well-known premium automobile brand that is generally seen as a symbol of speed, luxury, and wealth. Ferrari cars are built at the Maranello factory, which is 165,000 square meters in size. Ferrari has a rich racing history and is known for producing some of the fastest and most powerful cars in the world. The fastest street Ferrari is the F50GT1, which can go over 370 kph (about 222 mph). The most powerful Ferrari is the FXX, which has about 800 horsepower.
